--> Ingredients:
1. 1 cup of water
2. 1-2 tablespoons of coffee grounds (depending on your preference)
3. Sugar or sweetener (optional)
4. Milk or cream (optional)

--> Instructions:
1. Boil the water.
2. Add the coffee grounds to a coffee maker or French press.
3. Pour the boiling water over the coffee grounds.
4. Let it brew for about 4-5 minutes.
